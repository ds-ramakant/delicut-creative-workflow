DELICUT CREATIVE WORKFLOW — IMAGE SCORING GUIDE
================================================================================

SCORE DEFINITIONS
-----------------

  1 — REJECT
      Wrong direction entirely. Concept doesn't work.
      Action: Start over with a new prompt angle.

  2 — POOR
      Some elements work but major issues with composition,
      subject, or mood.
      Action: Significant prompt rethink required.

  3 — AVERAGE
      Correct direction but needs significant refinement —
      lighting, pose, setting, energy off.
      Action: Refine same prompt with specific fixes from notes.

  4 — GOOD
      Minor tweaks needed — small adjustments to colour,
      framing, or detail.
      Action: Light touch refinement, one more iteration.

  5 — APPROVED
      Ready for Figma handoff as-is.
      Action: Advance to Stage 5.


ADVANCEMENT RULES
-----------------
  Score 1–2  →  Rethink prompt angle entirely
  Score 3    →  Refine prompt based on your notes
  Score 4    →  One more light iteration
  Score 5    →  Moves straight to Figma handoff


HOW TO LEAVE FEEDBACK IN session_log.xlsx
------------------------------------------
  score   →  Enter a number 1–5
  notes   →  Be specific — mention what is wrong and what you want instead
             Examples:
               "lighting too dark, needs more energy"
               "pose is stiff, want more natural candid feel"
               "background too busy, simplify"
               "wrong body type, needs to be leaner"
  status  →  Write either: needs-refinement  /  approved
